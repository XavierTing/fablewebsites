Our Oven

A short history of Hilda

Every impossible pastry comes out of one impossible oven. Her name is Hilda, she runs at 180°C or 180°K depending on her mood, and this is her story.

01
The Finding

We found her in a collapsed observatory

Half telescope, half bread oven, entirely opinionated. The astronomers swore she'd been baking the constellations. We towed her home on a Tuesday that lasted nine days.

02
First Impossibility

The croissant that brought its own weather

Batch one came out raining. We panicked, then charged extra. Batch two came out snowing and won a small regional award for atmosphere.

03
The Inspection

We passed and failed the health inspection

The inspector opened the éclair box and became part of the paperwork. Officially we hold both a perfect score and a closure notice. We frame them side by side.

04
Today

Six impossibilities daily, warm at seven

Hilda preheats herself now. We just knead, fold, and apologize to the laws of thermodynamics. Come early — the queue occasionally loops.
