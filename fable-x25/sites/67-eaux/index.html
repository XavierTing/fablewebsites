no secrets.

transparency, but wearable.

“notes” are marketing. nouns are better. every bottle ships with its formula card — the naturals, the synthetics and the percentages, in plain language. yes, synthetics: musk hasn’t come from a deer since your great-grandparents were flirting, and we intend to keep it that way.

nothing here detoxifies you. nothing activates, awakens or unlocks. it’s perfume — alcohol, aroma molecules, restraint. it smells extremely good, and that is the entire promise.
