a teacher of the craft · 相不獨論
一

Read the whole相不獨論 — never one feature alone

No palace, no line decides anything by itself. A weak nose beside a bright forehead reads differently than a weak nose alone. The old rule: never judge a single sign in isolation.

二

Spirit over shape相由心生 — the face follows the heart

Manuals prize 神 (shén) — the light in the eyes, the ease in the face — above any measurement. A generous life re-draws its own map. The features are a starting line, not a sentence.

三

For reflection聊備一格 — hold it lightly

Take this as a mirror to think beside, not a fortune to obey. Nothing here is medical, and nothing is fixed. Read it, smile, and keep your own counsel.
