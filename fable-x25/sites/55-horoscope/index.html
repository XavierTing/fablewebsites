The Twelve at a Glance

THE FOUR & THE THREE

Elements & Modalities

Every sign is woven from two threads: an element — the substance of its temperament — and a modality, the manner in which it moves through the year.

△
Fire
Aries · Leo · Sagittarius

The kindling signs — warmth, will and appetite. Fire begins things, believes in things, and carries a room without meaning to.

▽
Earth
Taurus · Virgo · Capricorn

The steady signs — hand, harvest and hour. Earth tends what fire lights, and trusts only what it can hold, build or grow.

△
Air
Gemini · Libra · Aquarius

The wandering signs — word, weather and thought. Air carries ideas between people like pollen, and keeps the mind from settling too soon.

▽
Water
Cancer · Scorpio · Pisces

The deep signs — tide, memory and dream. Water remembers everything, feels before it thinks, and finds the low quiet places in every room.

CardinalThe Openers

Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn — the signs that begin each season. They initiate, instigate and set the wheel turning.

FixedThe Keepers

Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius — the signs at each season's heart. They hold, deepen and refuse to be hurried.

MutableThe Changers

Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces — the signs that close each season. They adapt, dissolve and prepare the ground for what comes next.
