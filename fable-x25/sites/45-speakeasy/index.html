The Address

There isn't one printed. Find the unmarked brass door on the lane behind the old print-works — the one with no handle. Knock twice, and ask for the owl.

The password changes with the moon. Tonight it is simply — ASK FOR THE OWL.

House Rules
  • No photographs. What happens here should only be remembered, imperfectly.
  • Speak at the volume of a secret. The room is small and listens well.
  • If you don't know what you want, say so. That's what the owl is for.
  • Last pour is called quietly. When the candles are snuffed, the night is over.

Keep a table

We hold a few seats in the back for those who ask nicely. Tell us when, and for how many.

The owl has your name. Come to the lane, knock twice, and it will be waiting.
