The Cinema One screen · Since 1974

BECOME A REGULAR

THE LUMEN opened its single screen in the autumn of 1974, in the shell of a shuttered variety theatre. We have never added a second screen and we never will. One room, one beam, one film at a time.

We programme what the multiplex forgot: restorations struck from original negatives, retrospectives of directors who deserved better, and midnight titles too strange to survive daylight. The projector is analogue when it can be, digital when it must be, and warm either way.

Members keep the light on. In return, we keep a seat for you — and the good coffee behind the bar.
