合婚 describes tendencies, never fates. A red thread was said to bind two people no matter the distance — but the old teachers were careful: the chart is a mirror held up to a temperament, and people choose. A 冲 is not a doom and a 合 is not a promise; the friction a chart names is simply the work a good pairing already knows how to do. Read this as a conversation-starter between two people — not a sentence passed on them.

陸推算之法How This Is Computed
生肖 · Zodiac

Year branch & its relations

Each person's animal is the 地支 of the birth year (turned at 立春, fixed to Feb 4). The pair is matched against the full tables: 六合 (secret friends), 三合 (trine allies), 相冲 · 刑 · 害 · 破.

日柱 · Day Pillar

From the Julian Day

The day 干支 = (JDN + 49) mod 60, the offset calibrated against known dates: 2000-01-01 = 戊午, 1990-05-20 = 乙酉. The day pillar — and each person's Day Master — is exact.

五行 · Elements

生 · 克 · 比和

Each chart's elements are tallied from its year and day pillars (four characters). The two are read for 相生 (one feeds the other), 比和 (alike), or 相克 (one checks the other) — and for whether one supplies what the other lacks.

纳音 · Sound

六十甲子纳音

Each birth year carries one of the thirty 纳音 — a poetic sound-element (e.g. 路旁土 Roadside Earth). The two are set in tune by the same 生 / 克 / 比和 logic.

A note on honesty: only the day pillar is exact to the day. The year branch turns at the true solar term 立春, approximated here by a fixed Feb 4 (near that boundary the animal may shift). Birth time is recorded but does not change these three readings — 生肖, 五行, and 纳音 all read the year and day, which the date alone fixes; the hour would only refine a fuller 八字. The compatibility score is one studio's weighting of these classical tests (生肖 45 · 五行 30 · 纳音 25) — a considered heuristic, offered for reflection, not an oracle.
