How the wheel knows your fate — the mathematics of stems, branches, and the five elements.

五行 The Five Elements

Every year carries one of ten Heavenly Stems (天干), which pair into five elements — each in a yang and a yin face. Your year's stem colours your animal: a Wood Rat and a Water Rat share a creature but not a temperament.

地支 Branches & the Cycle

Twelve Earthly Branches (地支) name the animals; ten stems and twelve branches interlock into the sexagenary cycle (干支) of sixty year-names before repeating. The wheel computes your animal as (year − 4) mod 12, and its element from (year − 4) mod 10.

The friendships and feuds between signs follow two ancient patterns:

三合 — the trines. Three animals, four months apart, form a harmonious triangle: your best allies. 六冲 — the clashes. The animal directly across the wheel is your opposite — the sign to handle with care.
