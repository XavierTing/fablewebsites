01Manifesto

READ TIME — 40 SEC

Form is a temporary agreement. Every mark we make is written in a material that remembers it can flow.

We don't ship artifacts. We ship instruments — identities that breathe, surfaces that listen, type that runs on simulation, not nostalgia.

MethodEverything begins as a field of forces. Brand, product, exhibition — we model the pressure first, and let the form condense out of it.
MaterialChrome is our honest material: it has no color of its own, only the color of its context. What you see in our work is mostly you.
MeasureA system is finished when removing one more element would make it stop moving. We work backwards from that point.

03Principles

FOUR, HELD LOOSELY
P—01

Motion before form

We decide how a thing moves before we decide how it looks. Stillness is a special case, and it has to be earned.

P—02

The surface is the system

No decoration layer. The visible skin of our work is the live output of the logic underneath — bend one, the other bends.

P—03

One accent, like a scalpel

A palette of silver and void, and a single ultraviolet line. If everything glows, nothing does.

P—04

Ship the uncanny

The version that feels slightly alive is the version that ships. Comfort is what the second studio you call will sell you.
