四君子 The Four Gentlemen

Four subjects, four seasons, one lifetime of practice. In sumi‑e a painter does not draw the orchid — she becomes patient enough for the orchid to appear. Each stroke is a virtue. Each virtue is a stroke.

Spring · 春

蘭

Orchid

Grace. She blooms where no one is looking and perfumes the valley anyway. A beauty that never asks to be seen.

Summer · 夏

竹

Bamboo

Integrity. Hollow at the heart, upright in the storm. It bends the whole way to the ground and refuses to break.

Autumn · 秋

菊

Chrysanthemum

Endurance. When the garden surrenders to frost, she opens. Persistence, it turns out, is a kind of flowering.

Winter · 冬

梅

Plum

Hope. The first blossom pushes through late snow, vermillion against white. Optimism, practiced annually.

The brush touches paper once. Everything before is preparation. Everything after is acceptance.
