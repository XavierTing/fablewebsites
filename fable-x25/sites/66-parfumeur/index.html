Pl. III — La commande

La Méthode

A bespoke accord is not bought; it is sat for, the way one sits for a portrait. Three visits, six weeks, one formula — archived in the house ledger under your name.

I.

La consultation

Ninety minutes at the organ with the perfumer. You smell in registers, not in perfumes — what your skin already carries, what it refuses, what it asks for.

90 min · à l’atelier · sur rendez-vous

II.

La composition

Three maquettes are struck from your accord and worn for a fortnight, in weather and in rooms. One returns to the bench for its final correction.

3 maquettes · 15 jours d’essai

III.

La maturation

The chosen formula rests six weeks in amber glass, turned weekly, tasted like a cellar wine — then filtered cold and bottled under your engraved name.

6 semaines · verre ambré · flacon gravé

La commission complète, de la première mouillette au flacon scellé —
