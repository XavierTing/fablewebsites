形Two schools, one plain idea

Feng shui (風水, "wind-water") is the older name for something ordinary: arrange a space so the good things about it are easy to reach and the awkward things are easy to avoid. Two traditions read it differently.

01 / 形势

形势派

Form & landscape

Reads the shape of things — where you enter, sightlines, what sits behind you when you sleep or work. Common-sense and observable. Most of this tool lives here.

02 / 理气

理气派

Compass & time

Reads direction and era with the 罗盘 (luopan) — a calculated layer where a building's facing and its construction period produce a chart. The Flying Stars below are this school.

03 / 分寸

分寸

A sense of proportion

Take what helps, leave what doesn't. An adjustment is a nudge — a lamp, a plant, a cleared corner — never a spell. Nothing here is meant to alarm you.

Reference · 九星

九星The nine flying stars

Each number is a star with a temperament and an element. Their nature shifts with the era: in the current Period 9 (2024–2043) the reigning star is 九紫; 八白 is the wealth star just past its peak; 一白 is quietly rising.
