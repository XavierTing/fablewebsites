Curatorial note

How a visit is hung

When you arrived, the gallery asked your browser for thirty-two bits of entropy. That number is your edition, and it seeds a deterministic random stream — a mulberry32 generator, for the record-keepers — which then makes every decision on these walls: which of six palettes to grind, where each particle is born, which cell is gilded, where the strata crack, how many ways the bloom repeats itself.

Nothing is stored and nothing is fetched. The same seed always hangs the same show — pass someone the edition link below and they will stand in this exact room. Press New Visit, and it never existed.
