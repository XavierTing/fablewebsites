HOUSE RULES

THE PRACTICE

I

No predictions

The deck does not tell the future. It corroborates the present. Every card you draw is a receipt for a decision already made, printed slightly after the purchase.

II

Shuffle honestly

The shuffling is for your benefit; the cards are not superstitious. They know where they are going. They have known, roughly, since you sat down at the table.

III

Draw again until honest

If a card contradicts what you already know, you dealt it wrong. Gather the spread, breathe, and ask again — the deck is patient, and you were always going to.
